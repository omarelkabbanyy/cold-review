# Cold Review

> A second opinion with no emotional investment.

Cold Review is an evidence-first AI prompt for early-stage businesses, apps,
products, services, and content projects. It researches similar projects,
measures structured similarity, explains what is genuinely unique, turns
competitor strengths into useful lessons, separates real gaps from guesses,
and gives a direct decision: **Kill, Pivot, Test, or Build**.

## What to give it

Paste the prompt into an AI with web research enabled, then describe:

- what you are making;
- who it is for;
- the problem it solves;
- how it works;
- evidence you already have;
- feedback you received;
- your time, money, skill, or distribution constraints.

The more concrete the evidence, the more reliable the verdict.

## What you get

- A direct cold verdict.
- Researched competitors with citations.
- A structured similarity percentage for each competitor.
- What is genuinely unique about every competitor and your project.
- What you can learn and ethically adapt from each competitor.
- How each lesson could strengthen your project.
- Facts, inferences, hopes, and unknowns separated clearly.
- Feedback decoded into signal, noise, and contradictions.
- A defensible market gap—or an honest admission that none was found.
- Practical ways to improve the project and expensive mistakes to avoid.
- The cheapest real test, a kill condition, and one next move.

## Use it

Open [`PROMPT.md`](PROMPT.md), copy the single-line prompt, paste it into your
preferred AI, and add your project description underneath it.

## Design principle

Cold Review is brutal toward the project and respectful toward the person.
It does not perform cruelty, invent research, or turn unsupported assumptions
into confident advice.
