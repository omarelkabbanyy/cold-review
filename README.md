# Cold Review

> A second opinion with no emotional investment.

Cold Review is an evidence-first AI prompt for pressure-testing early-stage businesses, apps, products, services, and content projects.

It instructs an AI system to examine evidence, research competitors when browsing is available, challenge unsupported assumptions, and finish with one decision:

- Kill
- Pivot
- Test
- Build

## How to Use

1. Open `PROMPT.md`.
2. Copy the complete prompt.
3. Paste it into an AI system.
4. Add a clear description of your project after the prompt.
5. Enable browsing when current competitor research is needed.
6. Independently verify important claims and citations.

## What to Include

For a stronger review, provide:

- what the project is;
- its current stage;
- the target user;
- the problem;
- the user’s current workaround;
- how the project works;
- why someone might switch;
- known competitors or alternatives;
- pricing or business model;
- distribution method;
- real evidence;
- evidence dates and sample sizes;
- usage and retention;
- positive and negative feedback;
- previous tests and failures;
- constraints;
- legal, safety, privacy, security, or regulatory concerns;
- the decision you need to make.

Write `unknown` or `none` rather than inventing information.

## Important Limitations

Cold Review has not been formally validated across every AI model.

Its results depend on:

- the selected AI model;
- browsing availability;
- source quality;
- citation accuracy;
- the information supplied by the user.

Similarity scores are analytical estimates, not objective facts.

Cold Review does not replace professional legal, financial, medical, safety, privacy, or security review.
