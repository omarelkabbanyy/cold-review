# Cold Review

> A second opinion with no emotional investment.

## What It Does

Cold Review instructs browsing-capable AI systems to research competitors and evaluate the evidence supplied by the user.

It evaluates an early-stage:
- business;
- app;
- product;
- service;
- content project.

It ends with one decision:
- KILL
- PIVOT
- TEST
- BUILD

## How to Use It

1. Open `PROMPT.min.txt` for the copyable version or `PROMPT.md` for the readable version.
2. Copy the prompt into an AI system.
3. Enable browsing when current competitor research is needed.
4. Complete the project input template below.
5. Paste the completed input after the prompt.
6. Independently verify important claims and citations.

## Project Input Template

```text
Project:

Project type:
[Business / App / Product / Service / Content / Other]

Current stage:
[Idea / Prototype / Launched / Revenue / Scaling]

Target user:

Geographic market:

Problem:

Current behavior or workaround:

How the project works:

Why someone might switch:

Known alternatives or competitors:

Pricing or business model:

Current distribution or acquisition method:

Evidence:

Evidence dates and sample sizes:

Usage and retention:

Feedback:

Previous tests and failures:

Constraints:

Legal, safety, privacy, security, or regulatory constraints:

Decision I need to make:
```

Write “unknown” or “none” instead of inventing information.

## What You Receive

A detailed, brutal, and evidence-based report. The output includes a competitor analysis, an evidence audit, decoding of feedback, identification of the real competitive gap, the biggest risk, actionable recommendations, and a final decision (KILL, PIVOT, TEST, or BUILD).

## Important Limitations

- Results depend on the AI model.
- Cold Review has not been formally validated across all AI models.
- Browsing may be incomplete.
- Citations may be wrong.
- Similarity scores are analytical estimates.
- The prompt does not replace legal, financial, medical, safety, or security review.
- Users must independently verify important decisions.
